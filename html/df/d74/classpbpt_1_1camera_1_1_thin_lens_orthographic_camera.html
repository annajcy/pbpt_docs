Detailed Description

template<typename T>
class pbpt::camera::ThinLensOrthographicCamera< T >

Orthographic camera with a finite circular aperture (thin lens).

Instead of originating rays from a fixed point behind the film, this camera simulates depth of field by sampling points on a circular lens and focusing rays on a plane at a given focal distance.
