enum class pbpt::camera::SphericalCameraMapping
strong

Mapping used by the spherical camera.

  • EqualRectangular: image coordinates map linearly to spherical angles (theta, phi), like a standard latitude-longitude environment map.
  • EqualArea: image coordinates are warped so that each pixel covers approximately equal area on the unit sphere.
