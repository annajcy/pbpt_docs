Detailed Description

template<typename T>
class pbpt::geometry::DirectionalCone< T >

Solid angle cone around a unit direction.

A directional cone is described by a unit axis vector and a maximum opening angle. It is primarily used to bound sets of directions for visibility or importance sampling.
