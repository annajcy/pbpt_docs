◆ RenderSpace

enum class pbpt::camera::RenderSpace
strong

Coordinate space where rendering computations are performed.

  • Camera: rendering is done in camera space; camera_to_render is identity.
  • CameraWorld: rendering space separates local camera rotation/scale from world translation (useful for improving numerical stability).
  • World: rendering is done directly in world space.
