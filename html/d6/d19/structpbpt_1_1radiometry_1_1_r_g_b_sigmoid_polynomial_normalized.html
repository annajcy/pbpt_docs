Detailed Description

template<typename T>
struct pbpt::radiometry::RGBSigmoidPolynomialNormalized< T >

Normalized quadratic sigmoid-polynomial for RGB basis spectra.

Similar to RGBSigmoidPolynomial, but defined over a normalized wavelength parameter t in [0,1], making the coefficients more stable across different wavelength ranges.

Evaluates the normalized sigmoid-polynomial at a wavelength.

The input wavelength is first mapped to a normalized range [0,1] and the quadratic polynomial and sigmoid are applied to that normalized parameter.
