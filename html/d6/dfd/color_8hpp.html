Project a reflectance illuminated by a given illuminant.

Integrates reflectance(lambda) * illuminant(lambda) * response(lambda) over wavelength, with optional normalization by the green channel.

Project a spectrum onto a 3-channel response.

Computes three channel values by summing spectrum(lambda) * response.channel(lambda) over the tabular wavelength range.
