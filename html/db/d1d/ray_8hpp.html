template<typename T>
T pbpt::geometry::safe_ray_tmax ( T dist)
inline

Compute a conservative t_max for a ray of length dist.

The returned value is slightly smaller than dist by an amount proportional to a small gamma factor, which helps avoid "overshooting" due to floating-point error when tracing to a known distance.
