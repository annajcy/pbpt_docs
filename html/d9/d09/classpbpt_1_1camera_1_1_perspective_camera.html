Detailed Description

template<typename T>
class pbpt::camera::PerspectiveCamera< T >

Ideal perspective (pinhole) camera.

Rays originate at the camera origin and pass through film points back-projected into camera space using the perspective projection.

Template Parameters
TScalar type (e.g. float or double).
