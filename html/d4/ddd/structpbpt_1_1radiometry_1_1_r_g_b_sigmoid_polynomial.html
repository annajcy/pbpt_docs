Detailed Description

template<typename T>
struct pbpt::radiometry::RGBSigmoidPolynomial< T >

Quadratic polynomial in wavelength with sigmoid shaping.

Evaluates a quadratic polynomial in wavelength and passes it through a sigmoid function to obtain a smooth, bounded spectrum.
