Detailed Description

template<typename T>
class pbpt::geometry::SurfaceInteraction< T >

Interaction with a surface, including geometry and texture coordinates.

Extends Interaction with:

  • geometric normal,
  • texture coordinates (u, v),
  • partial derivatives of the position with respect to u and v,
  • partial derivatives of the normal with respect to u and v.

This information is used for shading, texture mapping, and computing ray offsets that account for the local surface geometry.
