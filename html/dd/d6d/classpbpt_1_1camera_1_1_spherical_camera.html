Detailed Description

template<typename T>
class pbpt::camera::SphericalCamera< T >

Spherical camera that generates rays covering the full sphere.

The spherical camera maps film coordinates to directions on the unit sphere. Depending on the selected mapping, the sampling may be uniform in angle (equirectangular) or approximately uniform in solid angle (equal-area).
