Detailed Description

template<typename T, typename PixelSensorType>
struct pbpt::camera::RGBFilm< T, PixelSensorType >::Pixel

Per-pixel accumulation buffer.

Stores a running sum of RGB values (in double precision for better numerical stability) and the total weight of samples contributing to this pixel.
