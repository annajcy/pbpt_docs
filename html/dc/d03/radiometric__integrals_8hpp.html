const Func & func,
int sample_count,
RNG & rng )

Monte Carlo integration over a sampling domain.

Draws sample_count independent samples from domain, evaluates func(sample) for each, and accumulates the standard importance sampling estimator func(sample) / pdf. The final result is the arithmetic mean of these estimates.
