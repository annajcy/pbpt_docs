template<typename T>
Vector< T, 3 > pbpt::geometry::equal_area_square_to_sphere ( const Vector< T, 2 > & p)
inlineconstexpr

Map a point in [0,1]^2 to the unit sphere with equal-area property.

The mapping is designed so that each region of the unit square has the same area on the unit sphere, which is useful for stratified sampling of directions.

T pbpt::geometry::spherical_polygon_area ( const std::vector< Vector< T, 3 > > & vertices)
constexpr

Area of a spherical polygon on the unit sphere.

The polygon is assumed to be defined by vertices on the unit sphere in counter-clockwise order. It is triangulated fan-wise from vertex 0.

inlineconstexpr

Warp a 2D point into the unit square with equal-area tiling.

Points outside [0,1]^2 are mirrored back into the square so that the mapping preserves area when combined with equal_area_square_to_sphere.
