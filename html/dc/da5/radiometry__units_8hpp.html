◆ operator*() [1/3]

template<typename T, typename Tag, typename U>
auto pbpt::radiometry::operator* ( const RadiometryUnit< T, Tag > & quantity,
WaveLength< U > lambda )
inline

Multiplies a spectral quantity by wavelength.

This converts a per-wavelength quantity into the corresponding non-spectral quantity (for example, W/(m^2·sr·nm) times nm gives W/(m^2·sr)).

Only spectral tags are allowed; a runtime assertion checks this.
